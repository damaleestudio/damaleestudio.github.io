Damalee Studio was established in May 30 2015 with a team of talented photographers and cinematographers. Since then the business has grown and expanded and now Damalee Studio cover all areas of photography and cinematography throughout the Sabah. Damalee Studio's HQ is located in Kingfisher, Kota Kinabalu, while its branch is in peninsular Malaysia, Singapore & Brunei.
